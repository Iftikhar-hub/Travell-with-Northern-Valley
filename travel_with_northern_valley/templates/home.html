Best Time to Visit North

The northern areas of Pakistan Gilgit-Baltistan, offer some of the most breathtaking landscapes on earth. From blossom-covered valleys to snow-draped peaks, each season brings its own charm.

🌸 Hunza Valley

Best from April to October. Enjoy cherry blossoms in spring (March–April) and golden autumn in October, along with clear skies and mountain views.

🏔️ Skardu

Visit from June to September. Experience the surreal beauty of lakes, cold deserts, and the vast Deosai Plains during mild summer weather.

⛺ Fairy Meadows

Best from May to September. Ideal for camping and hiking with stunning views of Nanga Parbat and clear night skies.

🌉 Khunjerab Pass

Accessible from May to October. The high-altitude border crossing offers breathtaking views and is open only during snow-free months.

🌄 Phander Valley

Best from June to August. Enjoy the serene lake, trout fishing, and vibrant green scenery in peaceful surroundings.

🏞️ Rama Meadows (Astore)

Best from July to September. The meadows bloom with flowers and offer perfect weather for hikes to the stunning Rama Lake.

🎿 Naltar Valley

Visit from May to October for its colorful lakes and pine forests. Skiing is also popular in winter (December–February) with heavy snowfall.

🏡 Bagrot Valley

Best from April to October. A hidden gem known for traditional villages, green fields, and views of Rakaposhi.

🏰 Shigar Valley

Visit from May to September. Explore Shigar Fort and experience Balti culture amid dramatic mountain scenery.

🌊 Khalti Lake (Ghizer)

Best from June to August. Crystal-clear waters perfect for fishing and boating, surrounded by tranquil mountains.

🍎 Minapin & Rakaposhi Base Camp

Visit from May to October. Famous for trekking and apple orchards, with a stunning view of the Rakaposhi glacier.

🕊️ Yasin Valley

Best from June to September. Peaceful atmosphere, rich culture, and great trekking routes make it a perfect escape from crowds.
